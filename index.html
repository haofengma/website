Hello! Welcome to my academic website!

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Political Science in the Department of Political Science at The University of Iowa. My research areas are comparative politics and political methodology, focusing in particular on public opinion, political linguistics, race and ethnicity, and political geography.


I am currently writing my doctoral dissertation, which examines how the language that an individual speaks and the linguistic environment surrounding her shape the individual's political attitudes, utilizing the combination of multilevel models and spatial analysis. I expect the dissertation to be accomplished in April 2023 and I am now on the academic job market.
